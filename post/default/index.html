Granada On, the book

This book is about Granada, a living city that exists before, during and after the Alhambra, the paella and the shopping. It is about the graffiti, about the technology, about urban exploration and about stuff that is in plain sight and yet almost never seen.

It is about a city to be lived and walked and eaten and drunk. A city that has to be discovered over and over again.
